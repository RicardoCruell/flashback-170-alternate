Frequent Ask Questions

   - How do I change my password?
   A: Go to the menu drop down and select "Settings". Then, select "Change Password" and enter your new password.

   - How do I change my email?
  A: Go to the menu drop down and select "Settings". Then, select "Change E-mail" and enter your new email adddress.
